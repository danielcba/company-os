## Today's Progress

Today Company OS reached an important architectural milestone.

Until now, the Cognitive Lexicon was understood as a collection of concepts.

During today's work, a deeper structure emerged.

The Lexicon is becoming a cognitive system rather than a glossary.

---

## Discoveries

### The Ontology is the map.

The Cognitive Lexicon defines concepts.

The Ontology defines the universe where those concepts exist and interact.

---

### Concepts are not enough.

Concepts describe reality.

Principles explain why the cognitive universe behaves consistently.

This distinction led to the first draft of the seven Cognitive Principles.

Although not yet official, they establish the philosophical direction of Company OS.

---

### A concept requires a standard.

A new asset was designed:

`concept-template.md`

Every future cognitive concept will follow the same intellectual structure.

Consistency becomes part of the architecture.

---

### The first taxonomy appeared.

The Core Concepts naturally organized themselves into cognitive families.

Perception

Reasoning

Action

Learning

This suggests that Company OS is developing an internal grammar rather than an isolated vocabulary.

---

## Decisions

- Ontology will evolve gradually.
- Principles will only become official after validation.
- Core Concepts describe cognition, not software.
- Every new concept must belong to a cognitive family.
